I am Abel D Mathews, the Lead , Hack club PRC. We are a student tech club with the aim of improving coding culture in our ever-growing community. We have been hosting events and workshops round the year .

We are putting together the >HACK FLEDGE, a completely student-run hackathon which seeks to inspire and bring together young developers from all across the globe to build upon their ideas which will shape the next-gen technoverse

What is so great about our hackathon? Our hackathon provides a platform for budding developers from all across India , where they assemble in teams to learn, compete, interact and build up on their dreamt version of the world. With dedicated mentors, life altering sessions from professionals and a developer-friendly environment for first timers as well as seasoned learners, we aim to push them forward in their own journey to change the world to the better.

This year, we are going to conduct the Hackathon in Hybrid mode and we hope to attract hackers from all across India and expect the quality of projects to rise for what can be achieved in 48 hours.
